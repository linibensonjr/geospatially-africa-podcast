About Geospatially Africa

Africa's premier platform for geospatial conversations — from GIS and drones to machine learning and beyond.

Our Mission

Driving the African Geospatial Narrative

Geospatially Africa podcast started in April 2021 and was originally called Geospatially Podcast. We changed the name to Geospatially Africa podcast to make it more African-centric — a podcast that holds interesting conversations about the industry in Africa and around the world.

Currently one of the very few media outlets showcasing Afro-centric geospatial talents, expertise, and innovations globally, we strive to drive the narrative of the immense potential within the African geospatial industry, while exploring how it is contributing to Africa's individual and collective development.
